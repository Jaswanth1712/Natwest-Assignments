Discover Hyderabad

Explore the beauty and culture of this vibrant city

Birla Mandir

Birla Mandir, also known as Birla Temple, is a stunning marble temple dedicated to Lord Venkateshwara. It's situated on a hilltop, providing a panoramic view of the city. The intricate carvings and peaceful ambiance make it a popular place of worship and tourist attraction.

Chilukur Balaji Temple

Chilukur Balaji Temple, also known as Visa Balaji Temple, is a unique temple known for its belief in granting visas to devotees. It's one of the few temples dedicated to Lord Venkateshwara where no hundi (donation box) is present. Devotees often visit seeking blessings for their visa-related matters.

Charminar

Charminar is an iconic monument and mosque located in the heart of the old city. It was built in 1591 and stands as a symbol of Hyderabad's rich history. The four grand arches lead to bustling markets, and the panoramic view from the top offers a glimpse into the city's vibrant life.
